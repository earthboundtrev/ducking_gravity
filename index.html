Ready to Take Flight?

Welcome to Ducking Gravity

Ducking Gravity is an inclusive aerial arts community in Culpeper, VA. Whether you are seeking self-expression, a cross-training challenge, or just a unique, supportive community environment, we celebrate movement while promoting physical and emotional well-being, ensuring accessibility for all, regardless of ability or background.

Whether you are just starting out, or an experienced aerialist, our mixed level classes are designed to meet you where you are, and our coaches work with you specifically to help you define and reach your aerial goals.

What We Offer

Aerial Classes

Aerial classes for ages 3-8 on a membership basis, and ages 9-Adult on a membership or drop-in basis. Mixed level classes designed to meet you where you are.

Learn More

Open Aerials

Dedicated practice time for members of our community to work on skills they are learning in a supportive environment. Perfect for refining techniques and building confidence on the silks. Runs from 6:45 to 7:45 on Wednesdays.

Learn More

Birthday Parties

Celebrate your special day with a unique aerial arts experience! Professional instruction tailored to all skill levels, safe activities for kids and adults, and memories that will last a lifetime.

Learn More

Yoga Classes

Join us for Morning Vinyasa and Feel Good Flow yoga classes. These energizing and restorative practices combine breath with movement to support your aerial training and overall well-being.

Learn More

Pound Classes

Experience the power of Pound! Our high-energy fitness classes combine cardio, strength training, and rhythm using Ripstix® weighted drumsticks. Perfect for all fitness levels and a fun way to get your heart pumping while building strength and coordination.

Learn More

Special Events

Make your occasion unforgettable with a unique aerial arts experience! Perfect for corporate events, team building, bachelorette parties, and celebrations of all kinds with professional instruction and memories that will last a lifetime.
