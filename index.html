Ready to Take Flight?

Welcome to Ducking Gravity

Ducking Gravity is an inclusive aerial arts community in Culpeper, VA. Whether you are seeking self-expression, a cross-training challenge, or just a unique, supportive community environment, we celebrate movement while promoting physical and emotional well-being, ensuring accessibility for all, regardless of ability or background.

Whether you are just starting out, or an experienced aerialist, our mixed level classes are designed to meet you where you are, and our coaches work with you specifically to help you define and reach your aerial goals.

What We Offer

Aerial Classes

Experience the beauty and strength of aerial arts! Our aerial classes are designed for ages 3-8 on a membership basis, and ages 9-Adult on a membership or drop-in basis. Mixed level classes designed to meet you where you are, whether you're just starting out or looking to advance your skills. Build strength, flexibility, and confidence while learning fundamental silks techniques in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Learn More

Open Aerials

Open Aerials is dedicated practice time for anyone in our community who wants to work on their skills in a supportive environment. It's perfect for refining techniques and building confidence on the silks. New to aerials? You're welcome too! This is a great time to try silks and meet the Ducking Gravity community. Open Aerials runs Wednesdays from 7:00pm to 8:00pm.

Learn More

Junior Aerial Classes

Designed specifically for young aerialists who are ready to take their skills to the next level! These classes provide a fun, supportive environment where kids can build strength, flexibility, and confidence while learning exciting aerial techniques.

Learn More

Homeschool Classes

Designed specifically for homeschool students! Learn fundamental silks techniques and movements while building strength, flexibility, and grace in a supportive, age-appropriate environment. Connect with other homeschool students in a fun, active setting. Runs Tuesdays from 1:00pm - 2:00pm.

Learn More

Yoga Classes

Join us for Yoga! This energizing practice combines breath with movement to support your aerial training and overall well-being.

Learn More

Lyra Classes

Experience the artistry of aerial hoop! Our Lyra classes help you build strength, flexibility, and grace while learning this beautiful aerial discipline. Perfect for all skill levels.

Learn More

Mixed Apparatus

Move between lyra, silks, and ground conditioning in this perfect middle ground class. Learn new moves and shapes at your own pace in a low-pressure, creative environment. Guided by a coach, this class is more structured than Open Aerial and lighter than Foundations.

Learn More

Celebrate your special day with a unique aerial arts experience! Professional instruction tailored to all skill levels, safe activities for kids and adults, and memories that will last a lifetime.
