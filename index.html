About us

Ready to take flight?

An inclusive aerial community in Culpeper, built for every body and every level.

Welcome to Ducking Gravity

Ducking Gravity is an inclusive aerial arts community in Culpeper, VA. Whether you are seeking self-expression, a cross-training challenge, or just a unique, supportive community environment, we celebrate movement while promoting physical and emotional well-being, ensuring accessibility for all, regardless of ability or background.

Whether you are just starting out or an experienced aerialist, our mixed-level classes are designed to meet you where you are. Our coaches work with you to help you define and reach your aerial goals.

Programs

What we offer

Classes, camps, parties, and more—all in one welcoming studio.

Aerial Classes

Experience the beauty and strength of aerial arts! Our aerial classes are designed for ages 3–8 on a membership basis, and ages 9 and up on a membership or drop-in basis. Mixed-level classes meet you where you are, whether you are just starting out or looking to advance your skills. Build strength, flexibility, and confidence while learning fundamental silks techniques in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Learn More

Open Aerials

Open Aerials is dedicated practice time for anyone in our community who wants to work on their skills in a supportive environment. It's perfect for refining techniques and building confidence on the silks. New to aerials? You're welcome too! This is a great time to try silks and meet the Ducking Gravity community. Open Aerials runs Wednesdays from 7:00pm to 8:00pm.

Learn More

Junior Aerial Classes

Designed specifically for young aerialists who are ready to take their skills to the next level! These classes provide a fun, supportive environment where kids can build strength, flexibility, and confidence while learning exciting aerial techniques. Evening sessions run on a junior membership basis (with Spin and Swing on Wednesdays), and our coaches help families with placement so every child is in the right fit.

Learn More

Homeschool Classes

Designed specifically for homeschool students! Learn fundamental silks techniques and movements while building strength, flexibility, and grace in a supportive, age-appropriate environment. Connect with other homeschool students in a fun, active setting. Runs Tuesdays from 1:00pm - 2:00pm.

Learn More

Camps & Events

Seasonal camps, holiday programs, and one-off events gathered in one place. See flyers, dates, and prices, then jump straight to our calendar to register for the day that fits your family. Perfect when you want structured aerial fun without a long-term class commitment.

Learn More

Summer Camps

Weekly aerial arts camps for kids through the summer: Soar And Explore! mornings (ages 4–14), optional Directed Aerial Exploration! afternoons (ages 8–14), Stay All Day! for ages 8–14, plus optional before and after care (ages 8–14; June sign-up links on the summer camps page). Each track is booked by week in the calendar. Packing lists and schedules are on the summer camps page.

Learn More

Yoga Classes

Join us for Yoga! This energizing practice combines breath with movement to support your aerial training and overall well-being. Classes are relaxed and welcoming—follow a guided session with your own mat or one of ours. No prior yoga experience required.

Learn More

Lyra Classes

Experience the artistry of aerial hoop! Our Lyra classes help you build strength, flexibility, and grace while learning this beautiful aerial discipline. Perfect for all skill levels. Lyra Foundations is for ages 9 and up—learn mounts, poses, and transitions in a supportive, mixed-level environment.

Learn More

Mixed Apparatus

Move between lyra, silks, and ground conditioning in this ideal middle-ground class. Learn new moves and shapes at your own pace in a low-pressure, creative environment. Guided by a coach, this class is more structured than Open Aerials and lighter than Foundations.

Learn More

Birthday Parties

Celebrate your special day with a unique aerial arts experience! Professional instruction tailored to all skill levels, safe activities for kids and adults, and memories that will last a lifetime. Send a request through the birthday page so we can match your date, guest count, and ages to the right format—then we work with you on timing and details before the big day.

Learn More

Special Events

Make your occasion unforgettable with a unique aerial arts experience! Perfect for corporate events, team building, bachelorette parties, and celebrations of all kinds with professional instruction and memories that will last a lifetime. Tell us your group size, vibe, and any must-haves—we will work with you to shape something safe, fun, and truly one-of-a-kind for your guests.
