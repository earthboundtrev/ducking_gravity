Summer Camps 2026

Join us for weekly aerial arts camps: Soar And Explore! (ages 4–14, mornings), optional Directed Aerial Exploration! afternoons (ages 8–14), Stay All Day! (ages 8–14, 9am–3pm), plus optional before and after care (ages 8–14). The summer camps page has the flyer, packing list, and full schedule.

Before care (ages 8–14, 8–9am) is $30/week; after care (ages 8–14, 3–5:30pm) is $65/week when available. The week of July 6 is open for registration below; for add-ons and other dates, use our online calendar.
