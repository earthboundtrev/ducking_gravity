Soar The Sky with Baskets and Pie
One-year anniversary aerial showcase and celebration — picnic theme, open to the public.

Saturday, May 23, 2026 · 4:00–7:00 PM · At the Ducking Gravity studio — 15532E Montanus Dr, Culpeper, VA 22701 · Admission $5 (see calendar).

Celebrate our first year with aerial performances from our studio, a picnic-style gathering, and festivities. This event is open to the public. Bring a picnic dish to share; catch performances at 4:00 PM or 6:00 PM, with potluck picnic dining and activities around 5:00 PM. Raffles are open 4:00–7:00 PM.
