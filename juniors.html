About Our Juniors Classes
Our Juniors classes are designed specifically for young aerialists who are ready to take their skills to the next level! These classes provide a fun, supportive environment where kids can build strength, flexibility, and confidence while learning exciting aerial techniques. We offer two types of classes: • Junior Aerial Classes: Traditional aerial classes for young aerialists. Pricing: Junior membership or $75/month. • Spin and Swing Classes: Fun, dynamic classes that run every Wednesday from 5:00pm - 5:45pm. Pricing: $60/month or $15/class, Non-members $20/class. 🎪 What to Expect • Age-appropriate aerial skills and progressions • Building strength and coordination • Learning proper technique and safety • Fun, engaging activities that keep kids motivated • Supportive community atmosphere 🎪 What to Bring • Comfortable, form-fitting clothing (avoid zippers and buttons) • Water bottle to stay hydrated • Hair tie if you have long hair 👕 What to Wear • Leggings or athletic pants that cover the backs of your knees • Form-fitting top or tank top • Bare feet or socks • Avoid jewelry that could catch on the apparatus Please note: These classes are currently full, but we may expand capacity based on demand. Click the "Info!" button for each class date to learn more and join the waitlist if available.
