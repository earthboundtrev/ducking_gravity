What to Bring to Camp
Directed Aerial Exploration! campers (afternoons) should follow the same clothing, lunch/snack, water, and hair rules as Stay All Day! and other camp blocks. They will be working at height and across apparatus; coaches will meet them at their level. To help your camper have a safe, fun, and comfortable experience, please be sure they come prepared with the following: 🥤 Essentials A refillable water bottle (plastic or metal only -- no glass) A healthy lunch and snacks to fuel their day 👕 What to Wear Campers should wear: Snug-fitting bottoms that go past the knee A shirt that covers the midriff and can be tucked in ⚠ Important: Clothing must not have buttons, zippers, snaps, or Velcro. These can snag on the silks and may prevent your camper from participating in certain activities. Long hair needs to be tied back so it does not get caught in the silks! Some aerialists like to wear a shorty tank leotard with leggings and a t-shirt to slip on over it—this is a great option, but not required. Please note: The studio gets HOT! We do have AC in the front of the building, but dress accordingly and bring layers if needed. 💦 Water Play Alert! We plan to have outdoor water activities, so please pack: A change of clothes Crocs, flip-flops, or other shoes that can get wet.
