About Our Homeschool Classes
Aerial silks, also known as aerial fabric or tissu, is a beautiful and challenging aerial art form performed on long pieces of fabric suspended from the ceiling. Our homeschool silks classes are specifically designed for homeschool students, providing a perfect opportunity to build strength, flexibility, and grace while learning this captivating discipline in a supportive, age-appropriate environment. 🎪 What to Expect • Learn fundamental silks techniques and movements • Build upper body, core, and grip strength • Develop flexibility and body awareness • Progress at your own pace in a supportive environment • Connect with other homeschool students in a fun, active setting • No prior experience necessary 🎪 What to Bring • Comfortable, form-fitting clothing (avoid zippers and buttons) • Water bottle to stay hydrated • Hair tie if you have long hair 👕 What to Wear • Leggings or athletic pants that cover the backs of your knees • Form-fitting top or tank top • Bare feet or socks • Avoid jewelry that could catch on the fabric 📋 Class Details • Homeschool Class: Designed specifically for homeschool students. Runs Tuesdays from 1:00pm - 2:00pm. Pricing: Members $25 per class or $100/month, Non-members $30 per class. Please note: Aerial silks can be challenging on the hands and body, so be prepared for some initial discomfort as you build strength and calluses. All classes are designed to meet you where you are and help you progress at your own pace.
